  • ‘The Big Short’ Screenwriter Developing Drama Series with Amazon.

    Coming off of an Oscar win for “The Big Short,” screenwriter Charles Randolph has signed a drama development deal with Amazon Studios and is working on a series with the streaming service, Variety has learned. Randolph will develop an hourlong project, which is described as a multifaceted realistic look at what it would take to create a present day civil war in the United States of America. Aside from Randolph, no other creatives nor cast has been attached to the project.
